🔒 Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy
📅 Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Your privacy is as precious as a blooming flower. At Golden Garden, we are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we handle information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

1. Who We Are

Golden Garden (operated by Ornixa Moss Loft) is a gardening inspiration platform dedicated to helping beginners and enthusiasts grow thriving gardens. Our website address is: www.goldengarden.com. For any privacy-related inquiries, you can reach our Data Protection Officer at privacy@goldengarden.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better gardening resources, improve your experience, and maintain the security of our site. The types of data we gather include:

  • Personal Information You Provide: When you subscribe to our newsletter, leave comments, participate in contests, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and any details you voluntarily share.
  • Automated Information: Like many websites, we log data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and timestamps. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our content.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. For full details, please see our Cookies Policy.
  • Comments & User Contributions: If you leave a comment on our blog, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This allows us to recognize and approve follow-up comments automatically.
🌱 Our Golden Rule: We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your trust is the soil where our community grows.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To deliver personalized gardening tips, seasonal guides, and newsletter content you've subscribed to.
  • To improve our website’s performance, content, and user experience.
  • To respond to your inquiries, comments, or support requests.
  • To analyze trends and track usage patterns via aggregated data (never individually identifiable).
  • To protect the security and integrity of Golden Garden against fraud, spam, or other harmful activities.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our processing of your personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to our newsletter or accept optional cookies, you give explicit consent.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process data to improve our services, maintain site security, and analyze audience engagement, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Contractual Necessity: When you request a specific service (e.g., signing up for a workshop), we use your data to fulfill that request.
  • Legal Obligations: We may retain data when required by tax, record-keeping, or other regulatory frameworks.

5. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our website (e.g., hosting services, email marketing platforms, analytics). These parties are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and only use it for the services they perform.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, we may disclose information to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via prominent notice on our site.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Newsletter subscriber data is kept until you unsubscribe or request removal.
  • Comments and associated metadata are retained indefinitely to facilitate community discussions.
  • Analytics logs are anonymized or deleted after 26 months.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (subject to certain exceptions like legal obligations).

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you in a structured format.
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal constraints.
  • Restriction & Objection: Limit how we process your data or object to certain processing (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@goldengarden.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children’s Privacy

Golden Garden is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in certain regions). If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access our website from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a country where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. By using our site, you consent to such transfers. We implement appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, to ensure adequate protection.

10. Security Measures

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS), firewalls, and regular security audits to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🛡️ Proactive Gardening, Proactive Privacy: We regularly review our security practices and encourage you to use strong, unique passwords if you create an account on our platform.

11. Third-Party Links & Features

Our articles may contain links to external gardening suppliers, seed banks, or tool manufacturers. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We recommend reading their policies before providing any personal data. Additionally, we may use embedded content (e.g., YouTube gardening tutorials) which may collect data about you according to their own privacy policies.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the latest revision took effect. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of Golden Garden after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out using the following details:

  • 🌼 Email (Privacy Team): privacy@goldengarden.com
  • 📬 Postal Address: Golden Garden – Privacy Office, 123 Greenway Boulevard, Garden Heights, GH 56789, USA
  • 📞 Phone: +1 (234) 567-8901 (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm PT)
