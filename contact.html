🌿 Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a reply? +

Our team typically replies within 24 hours on weekdays. For weekend messages, we'll get back to you on Monday morning. We love reading each garden story!

Do you offer gardening consultations? +

Yes! We provide personalized virtual and in-person consultations. Simply select "Gardening Advice" as subject and mention your garden size & goals. Our experts will guide you.

Can I visit the garden or attend workshops? +

Absolutely! Golden Garden hosts monthly workshops from seed starting to pollinator gardening. Check our socials or email us for the upcoming schedule. All are welcome!

Do you accept plant or story submissions? +

We love featuring community gardens! Share your success stories or photos and we might feature them in our blog or Instagram. Use 'Feedback' subject and attach images.
