🍪 Cookies Policy

We use cookies to cultivate a better experience. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences — because your digital privacy grows with care.

🌿 We value your privacy. Golden Garden uses necessary cookies for site functionality and optional cookies to enhance your browsing, analyze traffic, and personalize garden tips. Do you agree?

📜 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our garden community, and improve our services. Cookies are completely safe and cannot carry viruses or harm your device.

🌱 At Golden Garden, we believe in transparency — just like healthy soil, clear information helps trust grow.

🍪 How we use cookies

We categorize cookies based on their purpose. Here’s how they help your gardening experience flourish:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for navigation, login, and security. You cannot disable these if you want the site to function.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us count visits, see which pages are most popular (like our plant library or contact form), and improve performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language or favorite plant categories, so you don’t have to set them again.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Occasionally used to show you relevant gardening offers or events based on your interests.

🛠️ How to manage or delete cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling necessary cookies may affect the functionality of our site — like remembering your favorite plant articles. Below are links to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome – Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari – Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox – Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge – Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Also, you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
